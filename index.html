Foreign Minister Mohammed bin Abdulrahman al-Thani said that Qatar will support peace proposal in the Middle East

February 21, 2020February 21, 2020 smdadmin Uncategorized

Foreign Minister Sheik Mohammed bin Abdulrahman al-Thani said that Qatar would consider any Middle East peace proposal that is reasonable to the Palestinians, noting that no US-led settlement can be placed on the Palestinians.

“As far as we see, right now there is a disconnect between the Palestinians and the U.S. Our position remains very firm: We are going to support any plan that the Palestinians are willing to accept,” he said in one of his interviews.

In an interview broadcast last week, senior advisor to the White House, Jared Kushner, said that the Palestinians earned “self-determination,” but stopped just short of endorsing Palestinian statehood and voiced doubt regarding their ability to govern themselves.

Al-Thani also encouraged Iran and the United States, during the press conference, to de-escalate tensions and find solutions.

“We believe that at one point there should an engagement – it cannot last forever like this. Since they are not willing to engage in further escalation, they should come up with ideas that open the doors,” Qatari Foreign Minister said.
